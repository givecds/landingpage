About CIOE

The China International Optoelectronic Expo (CIOE) is a worldwide renowned exhibition, which presents the most advanced optoelectronic technologies and innovations. CIOE 2016 will be held at Shenzhen Convention and Exhibition Center over a total exhibition area of 110,000 sq. meters. It will comprise five concurrent sub-expositions, focusing on optical communications & sensors, lasers & infrared applications, precision optics, LED and smart products. More than 3000 exhibitors are expected to attend this event and a four-day technical conference -China International Optoelectronic Conference (CIOEC) will be held concurrently.

Concurrent conference
The China International Optoelectronic Conferences (CIOEC) held concurrently with the world’s largest optoelectronic exhibition covers the latest technologies in the industry. The 2016 program includes:

  • Optical Communication Technology and Development Forum 2016
  • The Optoelectronics Global Conference 2016 (Academic Conference)
  • “Modern Optical Manufacturing Engineering and Science”(Shenzhen) International Conference 2016
  • 2016 IR Light Source Innovation Application Workshop
  • Intelligent Manufacturing Technology and Application Forum 2016
  • The 2nd International Forum on Sapphire Market and Technology

SHENZHEN TOURS

SHENZHEN- AMAZING CITY

Shenzhen is a booming metropolis in South China. The greater Shenzhen region is a major hub of China’s optoelectronics and consumer electronics production – hence, it is the perfect location for the world’s largest optoelectronics exhibition.

You’ll find fine dining from across China and from around the world. Also keep in mind that Shenzhen has excellent shopping and is famous for its golf, tennis and yachting, all with world-class venues and facilities. So get ready to work hard and have some fun.

Shenzhen is adjacent to Hong Kong so you may either fly directly to Shenzhen, or arrive via Hong Kong.

Shenzhen is easily accessible from Hong Kong by road, ferry or subway. For first-time travelers, it is most convenient to go from the Hong Kong International Airport to Shenzhen by limousine.
