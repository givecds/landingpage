About CIOE

The China International Optoelectronic Expo (CIOE) is a world-renowned event bringing the most advanced optoelectronics technologies and innovations to thousands of visitors annually. Established in 1999, CIOE celebrates 17 consecutive years of steady expansion and is now the largest show of its kind in the world. With a strong bond built over the years with companies and professionals in the field of optoelectronics, CIOE is committed to supporting the industry's continuous growth and technological development.

CIOE 2016 will be held at the Shenzhen Convention and Exhibition Center with a total area of 110,000 square meters, comprised of six concurrent specialized expositions focusing optical communications & sensors, lasers & infrared applications, precision optics, LED and smart products. More than 3,200 optoelectronic enterprises will exhibit their new products, while an extensive conference program will unveil the latest technologies and trends in optoelectronics.

SHENZHEN- AMAZING CITY

Shenzhen is a booming metropolis in South China. The greater Shenzhen region is a major hub of China’s optoelectronics and consumer electronics production – hence, it is the perfect location for the world’s largest optoelectronics exhibition.

You’ll find fine dining from across China and from around the world. Also keep in mind that Shenzhen has excellent shopping and is famous for its golf, tennis and yachting, all with world-class venues and facilities. So get ready to work hard and have some fun.

Shenzhen is adjacent to Hong Kong so you may either fly directly to Shenzhen, or arrive via Hong Kong.

Shenzhen is easily accessible from Hong Kong by road, ferry or subway. For first-time travelers, it is most convenient to go from the Hong Kong International Airport to Shenzhen by limousine.
